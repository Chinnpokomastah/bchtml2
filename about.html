Origin Story

Bob and George decided to make their own blend of thai and japanese food after studying abroad in both of those countries for their PhD's in nutrition science. They opened their first restaurant in New York City upon falling in love with the cuisine. They have since expanded to Tokyo, as well as Bob and George's home towns, Durham and Asheville respectively.
